Features

Self-hosting, minus the headache

ServerStick delivers a complete self-hosted stack with the simplicity of a USB stick. No DevOps degree required.

🔒

Privacy-First

SOPS-encrypted secrets with age keypairs generated on-device. Your data never leaves your hardware. No cloud dependency.

🤖

AI-Powered Setup

Pi, your local AI agent, scans hardware, recommends services, and configures everything — no manual editing required.

🌐

One-Click Tunneling

Pangolin tunnel gives your services public HTTPS URLs instantly. No port forwarding, no DDNS, no hassle.

💻

Runs Anywhere

Any Debian 12 machine — an old laptop, a mini PC, a cloud VPS. Plug in the USB, boot, and you're live.
